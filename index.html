BusMall Market Research

Instructions

Hello Participant, thank you for your time today. You will be shown a set of three products at a time in your browser for a certain number of rounds. In each set of products, please click on the product you would be most likely to purchase. Please keep in mind to answer honestly as your results and feedback will have an influence on which products could eventually be available for sale in the BusMall catalog. Thank you! After 25 clicks, you will be able to see a chart which provides the details of your choices.

Additional Options

As a participant, you also can look at the data what you have voted for. After the page refresh also you can view what you voted for. Please note that if you want to restart the votes, you need to clean up the local storage, and reset the page.

  1. Reset: Refresh the complete page.(Same as refreshing a page)
  2. Get Previous Data: This allows you to fetch the data which is available in the local storage. This will be available after the page refresh also.
  3. Clear Local Storage: Clean up the local storage.
